Resume
(pdf, updated September 2016)

I am a researcher working on interdisciplinary projects between computer graphics rendering and contemporary visual arts. I am interested in investigating the aesthetic aspects of computer-based rendering, and design new algorithms that render beyond photorealism. To apply the rendering technologies in an art-making context is part of my research. I also work as a photographer with digital cameras.

For the last few years, I worked in the Graphics Research Group in the computer science department of University of Victoria. I worked with Dr. Brian Wyvill, Dr. Amy Gooch and Prof. Lynda Gammon. I recently received my ph.D in computer science.

When there is a confusion about the pronunciation of my last name, I usually get called lucky.
